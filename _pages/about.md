I am a PhD student in the [School of Engineering, Mathematics, and Physics](https://www.kent.ac.uk/school-of-engineering-mathematics-and-physics) at the University of Kent, and a member of the [Statistical Ecology at Kent](https://research.kent.ac.uk/statistical-ecology/) group. My research area is in Statistical Ecology.

I completed my BSc in Mathematics (2020) and MSc in Computational Applied Mathematics (2021) at the University of Edinburgh before moving to the University of Kent to start my PhD.
